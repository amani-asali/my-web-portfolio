About Me

Amani Asali | Aspiring Interior Designer

I have always had a love for Art & Design and in particular Interior Design. Having achieved a number of Interior Design qualifications, I decided to persue a bachelor's degree in Interior Design, which I am on track to graduate with in 2021.

I always think of the end-user when designing my interiors, ensuring requirements are met and expectations are managed, and address feedback with each design iteration.

My skills in particular lie within my inherent ability to articulate my thinking through hand-drawings of interiors and exteriors, as well being able to create furniture from scratch based on the vision I have in mind.
